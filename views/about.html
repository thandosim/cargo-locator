About This Platform

This logistics web platform connects clients with available trucks across Southern Africa. The site helps streamline cargo transportation through real-time truck tracking, booking, and shipment management.

Key Features

  • Truck Dashboard: View all available trucks on a map and in a sortable table.
  • Cargo Management: List and view cargo shipments with origin, destination, and current status.
  • Truck Assignment: Assign available trucks to pending cargo directly from cargo detail pages.
  • Interactive Map: See live truck markers, click to view truck details or initiate reservations.
  • Client Information: Each shipment is linked to a client for easier follow-up and contact.

The goal is to reduce downtime for drivers, help businesses move goods efficiently, and provide a transparent overview of ongoing logistics.
